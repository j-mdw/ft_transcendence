PONG RULES - HOW TO PLAY IF YOU WERE NOT BORN IN 1972

Classic game

Each player has a paddle, well…, what should be a paddle, but is only a line. Player 1 controls his paddle with ‘w’ to go up and ’s’ to go down. Player 2 controls his paddle with ‘arrow up’ and ‘arrow down’ (I let you guess which one moves the paddle up…) The game is like a ping pong game, you have to send back the ball with your paddle. You’re the winner when you hit the fabulous score of 5 !

Rookie game

Your opponent is too strong ? You’re completely drunk ? Play the rookie mode ! Length of the paddle will be adapted to your drinking level.

Multiballs game

Long games are so boring ! Let put all the balls together to decide which player is the best.
